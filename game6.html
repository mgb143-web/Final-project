Overwatch/ Overwatch 2

Genre:First-Person Shooter

Why I like it:

Overwatch is a team-based first-person shooter where two teams compete in 6v6 or 5v5 matches (depending on the version) to complete objectives like capturing control points or escorting a payload. Instead of everyone playing the same kind of soldier, you pick from a roster of heroes, each with their own weapon, abilities, and role.


Heroes are divided into roles like Damage, Tank, and Support. Damage heroes focus on eliminations, Tanks protect the team and create space, and Supports heal and boost allies. Each map has a different layout and mode,some are control maps, some are payload escort maps, and some are hybrids. Maps are set in different locations around the world, like futuristic cities, snowy villages, or desert ruins. Every hero has an ultimate ability that charges over time and can change the flow of a fight when used well.


I like Overwatch so much because the heroes all feel unique, so switching characters can completely change how the game plays. The focus on teamwork makes wins feel really satisfying, especially when everyone coordinates their abilities and ultimates. Matches are fast and exciting, and it's especially fun when I'm playing with friends, calling out plays and trying different team comps until something clicks.
