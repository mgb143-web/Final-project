Persona 5/ Persona 5 Royal

Genre: Role-play/Story

Why I like it:

Persona 5 is a role-playing game where you play as a high school student in Tokyo who secretly lives a double life as a Phantom Thief. In the daytime, you go to school, answer questions, take exams, and hang out around the city. At night, you and your friends sneak into a supernatural world to fight enemies and change the hearts of corrupt adults.


The game mixes daily life sim and turn-based combat. You explore “Palaces,” which are dungeons based on a villain's twisted mind, like a castle, museum, bank, cruise ship, and casino. In battle, you summon Personas (spirit-like beings) to use elemental attacks, hit enemy weaknesses, and trigger All-Out Attacks. Outside of combat, you choose how to spend your time: building relationships with Confidants, doing part-time jobs, and raising social stats, which unlock new abilities and bonuses.


I like Persona 5 so much because it has a stylish look, a strong story, and a cool Phantom Thief theme that makes you feel like a rebel fighting for justice. The music is catchy, the characters feel real and memorable, and the mix of school life and dungeon crawling keeps things from getting boring. It's one of those games where I actually care about the characters and want to see what happens next.
