Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

Genre: Fighting

Why I like it:

Super Smash Bros. Ultimate is a fighting game where characters from tons of different video game series battle each other on stages based on their home games. Instead of using health bars, you try to knock your opponents off the stage by hitting them and raising their damage percentage.


The game has a huge roster of 89 fighters, including Mario, Link, Pikachu, Kirby, Sonic, Cloud, Joker from Persona 5, and many more. Each character has unique moves and playstyles, so learning them feels different every time. The stages are like arenas based on different games, Pokémon Stadium, Hyrule-type stages, Mario worlds, sci-fi platforms, and more with platforms, hazards, and sometimes changing layouts. There's also World of Light, a story mode where most fighters are captured and you travel across a big map, doing themed spirit battles to free them.


I like Smash Ultimate so much because it never gets old. There are so many characters and stages that every match feels a little different. It's perfect for playing with friends, whether we want serious 1v1 fights or crazy items-on, 4-player chaos. Even when I'm alone, World of Light and the huge roster give me a lot to do and experiment with, so I always feel like there's someone new to learn or a new combo to try.
